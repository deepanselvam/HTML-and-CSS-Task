About BMW Car

BMW, or Bayerische Motoren Werke, is a German automobile manufacturer renowned for producing luxury vehicles that combine performance, innovation, and elegant design. Founded in 1916 , the company has built a legacy centered around its slogan "The Ultimate Driving Machine," reflecting its focus on precision engineering and driving pleasure. BMW's lineup ranges from compact sedans and SUVs to high-performance M series models and increasingly popular electric vehicles under the "i" sub-brand.
